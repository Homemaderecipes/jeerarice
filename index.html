Jeera rice recipe

how to make jeera pulao with step by step 

using cooked basmati rice:
firstly, soak ½ cup basmati rice for 20 minutes.
now in a large vessel boil 4 cups of water.
once the water comes to a boil, add in soaked rice along with 1 tsp oil and ½ tsp salt.
mix well and boil for 8 minutes.
boil until rice gets cooked completely.
drain off the water from basmati rice.
and pour 1 cup of cold water to prevent from further cooking. keep aside for 15 minutes.
now in a large heat 1 tsp ghee and splutter 1 tsp cumin seeds.
now add in cooked basmati rice. make sure the water is drained off completely.
also add ¼ tsp salt and mix gently.
finally, add 2 tbsp coriander leaves and enjoy jeera rice with dal.
firstly, in a cooker heat 1 tsp ghee and splutter 1 tsp cumin seeds.
now add ½ cup basmati rice (soaked 20 minutes) and saute for a minute.
add 1 cup of water, ¼ tsp salt and mix well.
cover and pressure cook for 2 whistles on medium flame.
once the pressure settles down, open the cooker and mix gently.
finally, add 2 tbsp coriander leaves and enjoy jeera rice with dal.



notes:
firstly, use good quality basmati rice to get extra long grains of rice.
also, adding ghee / oil while cooking rice will make rice non sticky.
additionally, add onions and other spices to make jeera rice more flavourful.
finally, jeera rice recipe tastes great when prepared with fresh homemade ghee.
